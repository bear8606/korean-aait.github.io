Introduce

Hangeul is the Korean character. Hangeul was create in 1443 by King Sejong and scholars at Jiphyunjeon (Hall of the Assembled Sages). Prior to the creation of Hangeul, spoken Korean was transcribed using Chinese characters. However, because Chinese characters were difficult for the common people to use, access to the Chinese transcription system was limited only to the privileged classes. Hangeul was invented to enable ordinary people to express their ideas in writing.
